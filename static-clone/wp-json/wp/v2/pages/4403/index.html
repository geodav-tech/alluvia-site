30-Day Return & Exchange Policy<\/span><\/h2>\n

We allow returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery on all purchases unless otherwise posted as a \u201cno return\u201d or \u201cfinal sale”. This policy only applies if the product is clean, dry, unmodified, undamaged and unused. Any product returned that does not follow the criteria previously mentioned will result in forfeiture of at least 50% of the total refund price. The returned product must be insured during shipment, with the insurance and shipping charges prepaid by the buyer. If this product is returned uninsured, the buyer assumes all risks of loss or damage during shipment.<\/p>\n
